THE MATRIX ORIGIN

Founded in 2023 by Matome Rambau and Ntshidiseng Mofokeng, Matrix Analytics emerged from a shared vision to revolutionize financial services through technology.

With combined experience at leading audit firms including SNG Grant Thornton, KPMG, and EY, our founders identified a critical gap in the market - the need for accounting solutions that leverage cutting-edge data analytics while maintaining rigorous compliance standards.

Today, Matrix Analytics stands at the intersection of financial expertise and technological innovation, serving clients across South Africa with our unique blend of traditional accounting rigor and futuristic data solutions.
